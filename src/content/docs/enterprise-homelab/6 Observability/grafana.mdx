## Role in the Homelab

**Grafana** will provide dashboards on top of **Prometheus** metrics: host health, network utilization, and service availability across the homelab.

## Planned Scope

* Infrastructure overview dashboards (Proxmox, switches, Tailscale node).
* Alerting integration with notification workflows from the Local SRE Platform.

## Dashboards & Alerting

Dashboard designs and alerting rule definitions will be added here once Prometheus is live.

Not yet deployed.
